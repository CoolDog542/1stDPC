About Us

Diversified Plastics Corporation is a custom molder of all types of plastics, from expandable foam co-polymers to injection grade plastics. DPC was founded in 1969 and has been in continuous operation ever since. Today its facilities encompass three of the largest state-of-the-art molding factories in the United States. Since its beginning, DPC has been a leader of plastics industry in the fields of innovations, quality molding, setting standards for service and timeliness, as well as developing cutting edge technology.
